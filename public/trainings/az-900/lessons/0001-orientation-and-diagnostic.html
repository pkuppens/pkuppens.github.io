Orientation & Diagnostic

You already know cloud computing fundamentals. This course isn't teaching you what a VM or object storage is — it's mapping what you already know onto Microsoft's terminology, then drilling the exam's trick patterns. Take this 16-question diagnostic across all three domains to see where you stand.

How this course works Four short lessons (one per domain cluster), five 30-question mock exams at exam weighting, and two printable references — an exam blueprint and a glossary with a dedicated cross-cloud terminology table. Aim for ≥85% before booking the exam.

The three domains

The trap to watch for Most AZ-900 wrong answers aren't wrong because you don't understand the concept — they're wrong because you picked a non-Azure name instead of the Azure one, or the retired Azure name instead of the current one.
